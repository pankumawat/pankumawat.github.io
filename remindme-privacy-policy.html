Privacy Policy for RemindMe

Effective Date: 12 February 2026

RemindMe respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the RemindMe mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

RemindMe does not collect, store, process, or share any user-related data, including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • IP address

All reminders and related data remain entirely on your device.

2. How the App Works

RemindMe works without requiring account creation or sign-in. You can use the app without providing any personal details. All reminders are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

RemindMe does not use:

  • Third-party analytics tools
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Cloud storage or backend servers

No data is shared with any third party.

4. Permissions

Any permissions requested by the app (such as notification access) are used only to enable core app functionality. These permissions are not used to collect, track, or transmit user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

RemindMe does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected, no personal information is stored or shared.

6. Data Security

Because RemindMe does not collect or store personal data, there is no user data stored on our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be made available through the app or its official listing. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

LASER CLASS IV

Laser therapy uses specific wavelengths of light (red and near-infrared) to create therapeutic effects to the nerve, muscle tendon and ligaments.

Class 4 laser therapy stimulates the production of ATP (adenosine triphosphate) which drives many of the processes within our cells and speeds up the healing process within cells.

The benefits of class 4 laser therapy include reduced pain and swelling as well as improved range of motion.
